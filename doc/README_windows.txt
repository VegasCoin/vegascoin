Intro
-----
VegasCoin is anonymous internet money that can be instantly transferred to anyone in the world over a public peer-to-peer network ran by volunteers .


Setup
-----
Unpack the files into a directory and run vegascoin-qt.exe.

VegasCoin-Qt is the original VegasCoin client and it builds the backbone of the network.
However, it downloads and stores the entire history of VegasCoin transactions;
depending on the speed of your computer and network connection, the synchronization
process can take anywhere from a few hours to a day or more.
